About our Foundation

Lend your strength and love to the less fortunate

Tei Wa Mbau Foundation is a non-profit organization rooted in the values of compassion, integrity, and collaboration. Our team is composed of passionate individuals who share a common goal: to uplift lives and make a positive impact. Together, we work tirelessly to create pathways for growth and empowerment, driven by a deep belief in the potential for change.
