About Us

About Our Foundation

The Tei Wa Mbau Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by Patrick Malika Mbau, a prominent businessman and senate candidate for Makueni county 2022 . The foundation's name, "Tei Wa Mbau," is derived from Kamba heritage and translates to "Mercy and power of 20 bob."

The primary mission is to empower communities and transform lives, with a special focus on the Makueni County in Kenya. Patrick Mbau's deep commitment to giving back to the community that raised him led to the establishment of this foundation.

At Tei Wa Mbau Foundation, we believe in the transformative power of education, community development, and sustainable growth.

Mission

To create positive change and empower individuals, families, and communities to rise above challenges and unlock their full potential. With a steadfast commitment to making a difference, we are dedicated to fostering a better future for all.

Vision

Our vision is a world where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances. We envision vibrant and self-sufficient communities that are equipped with the tools they need to achieve sustainable progress

Who We Are

Tei Wa Mbau Foundation is a non-profit organization rooted in the values of compassion, integrity, and collaboration. Our team is composed of passionate individuals who share a common goal: to uplift lives and make a positive impact.
