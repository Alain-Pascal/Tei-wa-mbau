About Tei wa mbau

The Tei wa mbau foundation is a non-profit organizatin founded by Patrick Malika Mbau, a prominent businessman and senate candidate for Makueni county.With a deep commitment to giving back to the community that raised him, Patrick Mbau has established this foundation to empower villages, schools, and the entire county. The oundation ocuses on providing access to clean drinking water, quality education, and economic opportunities for bychild.

One of the flagship projects of the foundation is the provisin of a school bus fr st peter's Kyamutheu, Patrick Mbau's former school, where he also serves as a board member. This initiative aims to enhance transportation system for students, ensuring their and safe reliable access to education.

Who are we?

Tei Wa Mbau Foundation is a non-profit organization rooted in the values of compassion, integrity, and collaboration. Our team is composed of passionate individuals who share a common goal: to uplift lives and make a positive impact. Together, we work tirelessly to create pathways for growth and empowerment, driven by a deep belief in the potential for change.

Our vision

Our vision is a world where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances. We envision vibrant and self-sufficient communities that are equipped with the tools they need to achieve sustainable progress.

Our mission

Our mission is to create positive change and empower individuals, families, and communities to rise above challenges and unlock their full potential. with asteadast commitment to making adifference, we are dedicated to fostering a better future for all.
